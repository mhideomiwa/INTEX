Social Media and Mental Health Resources

Improvement Tips:

Intention

Establish a daily phone usage curfew

Have a purpose when getting on social media

Leave your phone in another room

Evaluate if you are a passive or active user

Prioritize interacting with people face-to-face

Look to more news sources to stay informed

Phone

Set your phone to grayscale

Use time limits

Track the time you spend and set goals

Disable push notifications

Take social media apps off your smartphone

Mute or unfollow accounts

Change the location of the apps on your phone

Health

Join a club

Interact with strangers

Express gratitude

Practice mindfulness

Participate in volunteer work

Get outside
